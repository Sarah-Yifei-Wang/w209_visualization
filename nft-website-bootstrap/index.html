Introduction to Non-Fungible Tokens

NFTs are digital assets that represent real-world objects like art, music, in-game items, and videos. The ownership of this digital collectible is recorded on a blockchain that cannot be replicated. Owning an NFT is analogous to owning an original physical piece of art. NFTs can be bought and sold in different online Marketplaces and are primarily purchased with cryptocurrencies.
